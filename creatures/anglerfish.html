AnglerFish

Anglerfish are deep-sea predators known for their bioluminescent lure, which they use to attract prey in the dark, abyssal depths of the ocean.ions.

Interesting Facts

  • The anglerfish's most distinctive feature is a modified fin ray that acts as a lure to attract prey. This ray, called an esca or illicium, glows due to symbiotic bacteria that live in and around the fish
  • Anglerfish have large mouths and flexible bodies that allow them to swallow prey that's up to twice their size. They have hinged teeth for biting and puncturing prey, and a second row of smaller teeth for breaking up the skin
