Clownfish

Clownfish are vibrant reef dwellers known for their symbiotic relationship with sea anemones, offering mutual protection in the bustling ecosystems of coral reefs.

Interesting Facts

  • Clownfish are not just orange: Clownfish can be black, yellow, or red in addition to orange
  • Clownfish are born male: All clownfish are born male, but can change sex to become the dominant female in a group.
  • Clownfish live with anemones: Clownfish live in a symbiotic relationship with anemones, where the anemone protects the clownfish from predators and provides food scraps. In return, the clownfish attract other fish to the anemone with its bright colors
