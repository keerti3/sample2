Jupiter

Jupiter, the largest planet in our solar system, is known for its breathtaking beauty and impressive size. It's a gas giant with a colorful and dynamic atmosphere. Jupiter is home to the Great Red Spot, a massive storm that has raged for centuries. It has more than 70 known moons, including the largest moon in the solar system, Ganymede.

Jupiter is a fascinating celestial body, and its exploration by spacecraft has provided valuable insights into the outer planets of our solar system. Learn more about this remarkable world in the sections below.
